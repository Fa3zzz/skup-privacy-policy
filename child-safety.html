Child Safety Standards & Trust and Safety Policy

Effective Date: 9 May 2026

Sküp maintains a strict zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse, exploitation, grooming, trafficking, predatory conduct, and any activity that endangers minors.

The Platform is designed for adults and operates under strict safety and enforcement standards intended to detect, prevent, investigate, remove, and report exploitative or harmful conduct involving minors.

1. Zero-Tolerance Child Safety Policy

The following conduct is strictly prohibited on Sküp:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Sharing, requesting, distributing, or promoting sexual content involving minors
  • Grooming or predatory behavior
  • Sexual exploitation or coercion involving minors
  • Trafficking or solicitation involving minors
  • Harassment or inappropriate interactions involving minors
  • Attempts to exploit, manipulate, threaten, or endanger minors
  • Use of the Platform to facilitate unlawful conduct involving minors

Any suspected violation may result in immediate enforcement action without notice.

2. Enforcement Actions

The Company reserves unrestricted rights to:

  • Remove content immediately
  • Suspend or permanently terminate accounts
  • Restrict device or IP access
  • Preserve evidence and records
  • Escalate emergency threats
  • Cooperate with law enforcement and child protection agencies
  • Disclose information where legally required or reasonably necessary for safety

The Company may preserve:

  • IP addresses
  • Device identifiers
  • Messages and metadata
  • Account activity logs
  • Deleted content
  • Moderation and enforcement records

3. Reporting Mechanisms

Users are strongly encouraged to report:

  • Grooming attempts
  • Predatory conduct
  • Exploitative or suspicious interactions
  • Content involving minors
  • Trafficking or coercion concerns

Reports may be submitted through in-app reporting systems or by contacting:

Email: support.skup@gmail.com

The Company may review reports, investigate conduct, preserve evidence, and take enforcement action at its sole discretion.

4. Age Restrictions

Sküp is intended strictly for users who are:

  • 18 years of age or older; OR
  • The age of legal majority or digital consent in their jurisdiction, whichever is higher.

The Platform is not intended for children.

The Company does not knowingly permit underage users to access or use the Platform.

If the Company reasonably believes a user may be underage, the Company reserves the unrestricted right to:

  • Suspend or terminate accounts
  • Restrict access to the Platform
  • Request age verification
  • Remove content
  • Preserve records for legal or safety purposes

5. Automated Detection & Safety Systems

The Platform may implement:

  • AI-assisted moderation systems
  • Automated behavioral analysis
  • Keyword detection technologies
  • Content scanning systems
  • Fraud and exploitation detection tools
  • Manual moderation systems
  • Repeat offender tracking
  • Device and IP restrictions

Users acknowledge that:

  • Automated systems may not detect all harmful conduct
  • Moderation systems are inherently imperfect
  • False positives or false negatives may occur

Failure to detect, prevent, or remove harmful content shall not create liability for the Company to the maximum extent permitted under applicable law.

6. Law Enforcement Cooperation

The Company reserves the unrestricted right to:

  • Preserve user records and metadata
  • Disclose user information where legally required
  • Report suspected illegal activity
  • Cooperate with governmental authorities, regulators, and child protection organizations
  • Respond to lawful requests, subpoenas, or emergency disclosure requests

Users acknowledge that anonymity protections may be limited or overridden where legally required or reasonably necessary to protect minors or public safety.

7. Platform Status & Limitation of Responsibility

Sküp operates solely as an intermediary technology platform that enables user-generated interactions and communications.

The Company:

  • Does not create user content
  • Does not verify all user identities
  • Does not monitor every interaction or communication
  • Does not guarantee detection of all harmful activity
  • Does not guarantee user safety

Users remain solely responsible for their conduct, communications, and interactions on and outside the Platform.

8. Emergency Situations

If you believe a child is in immediate danger, contact your local law enforcement authorities or emergency services immediately.
