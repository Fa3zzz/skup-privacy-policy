Privacy Policy for Sküp

Effective Date: April 8th, 2026

Sküp is built to enable real conversations between users. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address
  • Profile information (display name, optional photo)
  • User-generated content (messages, chats)
  • Usage data via Firebase

2. How We Use Information

  • Account creation and authentication
  • Enable communication between users
  • Send essential emails
  • Improve app performance

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using Firebase services.

Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary to provide our services. When you delete your account, your personal data is deleted or anonymized from our systems within a reasonable timeframe, except where retention is required by law.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data.

5. User Content

Content you create may be visible to other users.

Safety

Users can report inappropriate behavior and exit conversations at any time.

6. Your Rights

You can delete your account directly within the app. You may also request deletion by contacting support.skup@gmail.com.

7. Changes

We may update this policy.

8. Contact

support.skup@gmail.com

Account Deletion

You can delete your account directly from within the Sküp app. If you need assistance, you can also request deletion by contacting: Support.skup@gmail.com

Child Safety Standards

Sküp has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

We strictly prohibit any content or behavior that involves the exploitation, abuse, or endangerment of minors. This includes:

  • Sharing or requesting sexual content involving minors
  • Any form of grooming or predatory behavior
  • Harassment or inappropriate interaction involving minors

Enforcement

Any user found engaging in such behavior will be permanently banned and may be reported to relevant authorities.

Reporting

Users can report inappropriate behavior through the app’s reporting option or by contacting us at support.skup@gmail.com. We review all reports and take appropriate action.

Age Restrictions

Sküp is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly allow minors under this age to use the platform.

Compliance

We comply with applicable laws and cooperate with law enforcement when required.

If you believe a child is in immediate danger, please contact your local authorities.
